Indian-cucumber-salad

  • INGREDIENTS

  • PREPARATION

STEP 1

Chopped cucumbers are tossed with a tempering made of ghee/oil, mustard seeds, cumin seeds, green chilies, hing and curry leaves

STEP 2

It is further mixed with crushed peanuts, salt, sugar (optional), coriander leaves and lemon juice.If you like you can also add fresh coconut (raw coconut).
